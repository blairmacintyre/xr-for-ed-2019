<h2>What is XR?</h2>
  <p>VR = Virtual Reality </p>
  <p>AR = Augmented Reality (augment perception)</p>
  <p>MR = Mixed Reality (spectrum from AR to VR) </p>
  <p>XR = Cross Reality, Extended Reality, ... </p>
  <br>
  <p>XR = xR = "all of the above"</p>
